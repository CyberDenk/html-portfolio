The Best Movies According To Me

My top 3 movies of all time.

Hawksaw Ridge

This is one of the criziest and most beautiful war story that did happen, this what a human is supposed to do in his existence, pure heartful gestures.

Pirates of the Caribbean

This one is not for only the story (even tho it's good too) but it's a mix of everything: the orchestra, the placement of these compositions, the sets, the costumes, the feeling to believe these people are at this point of time, i mean everything is too good, chills.

How to Train your Dragon

This is clearly my favorite animation movie that has been made up til today: the main theme being 2 different melodies that gives you the best feeling when played together (test drive scene OMG), and just nailed toothless acting/personnification of the friendliest and most loyal entity to a human, just wanna cry thinking about it.
